Hello! My name is Piotr Modzelewski. I am graduate of the Faculty of Physics at Warsaw University of Technology, where I was studying Computer Physics and then Nuclear Physics. During my studies I was working within Heavy Ion Reaction Group in field of particle physics in A Large Ion Collider Experiment located at Large Hadron Collider in CERN.

Web development was my interest for a long time. In 2006 I have created my first amateur website in pure HTML and CSS. Nowadays, my aim is to work as a professional web developer. I am trying my best in extending my front-end as well as back-end skills.

This site was created to share my top favourite hobbies with you. If you are interested in them as well, please, do not hesitate to ask me anything. You can contact me via e-mail. Enjoy!

Cycling

Cycling is my favourite sport activity in the fresh air. It is entertaining and very healthy way of spending free time. I do enjoy all kinds of bicycle trips: the trips in a big city and the ones in a country side. The short, highly-intensive runs and the long ones at a moderate pace. The lonely rides when I have a time for think over some issues as well as the journeys with friend when we can chatter to each other. In my opinion, the bicycle is the best choice to explore new places in your neighbourhood.

CERN

European Organization for Nuclear Research (CERN) is a scientific centre which provides infrastructure, accelerator complex, and global computing resources needed for studying the high-energy collisions of particles. Experiments established within it focus on the exploration of fundamental laws that govern our world. The institution has a significant contribution into the improvement of particle acceleration and detection techniques, development of positron emission tomography, and the invention of World Wide Web information space. CERN is an impressive example of the greatness of human mind that lead to the discovery of how the world is constructed.
